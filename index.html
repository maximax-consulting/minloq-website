FAQ

Common questions.

Which Singapore banks does Ben support?
Ben reads PDF and CSV statements from DBS/POSB, OCBC, UOB, Maybank, Aspire, Wise and ANEXT. CSV export works for most other banks. Contact us if your bank isn't listed.
Do I need to set up Xero bank feeds?
No. Upload PDF or CSV bank statements directly via the portal. Xero integration is available on Firm and Growth plans for those who want it — but it's optional, not required.
Is the output audited or reviewed by Minloq?
No. All outputs are AI-assisted compilations prepared from information provided by management. No audit or review has been performed by Minloq. Management and their accountant remain responsible for accuracy. Every output carries a compilation notice.
Can multiple staff submit documents?
Yes. Register multiple staff numbers per company. Ben identifies who is submitting from their registered number and routes documents to the correct client queue automatically.
How does Minloq handle missing receipts?
Ben records what exists and flags what is missing — with clear notes rather than silent gaps. Clients are prompted to reconstruct or declare missing items. Ben knows Singapore documentation thresholds and guides accordingly.
What languages do the bots support?
All bots respond in the language you type — English, Mandarin, Bahasa Melayu, Tamil or Singlish. Regulatory references (IRAS, ACRA, CPF) remain in their official form.
Is my data secure and PDPA compliant?
Yes. All data is hosted in Singapore (AWS ap-southeast-1). Each subscriber's data is completely isolated using row-level security. Your data is never used to train AI models. Minloq is PDPA compliant.
Can I import my existing chart of accounts?
Yes. Import your COA via CSV from Xero, QuickBooks or any standard format. Ben learns your account mappings over time — the more you use it, the fewer manual corrections you need.
Is there a founding discount for accounting firms?
Yes — we have 10 founding firm slots at 50% off for the first 3 months. These are for firms willing to provide feedback during our early access period. Contact us to check availability.
What accounting software does Minloq work with?
Xero and QuickBooks Online on Firm and Growth plans. For SME and SSME plans, Ben generates a formatted Excel bookkeeping file. Standalone mode — no external accounting software needed.
