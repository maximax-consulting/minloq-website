Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Minloq Pte. Ltd. (UEN 202615062H)

1. Who we are

Minloq Pte. Ltd. (UEN 202615062H), 73 Ubi Road 1, #09-51, Singapore 408733, operates the Minloq platform accessible at minloq.sg. We are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA) of Singapore.

2. Data we collect

We collect information you provide when signing up and using the platform, including your name, email address, company name and UEN, financial documents uploaded to the platform (bank statements, invoices, receipts), and usage data such as log files and session information.

3. How we use your data

Your data is used solely to provide the Minloq service — to process documents, generate compliance outputs and operate the platform. We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data to train AI models.

4. Data storage and security

All data is stored in Singapore (AWS ap-southeast-1) in compliance with PDPA data residency principles. Each subscriber's data is isolated using row-level security. Access is restricted to authorised personnel only. Documents are stored in private, access-controlled storage.

5. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as required by Singapore law. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing hello@minloq.sg. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

6. Third-party processors

We use the following third-party processors to operate the platform: Amazon Web Services (document processing and storage, Singapore region), Supabase (database, Singapore region), Vercel (application hosting). All processors are bound by appropriate data processing agreements.

7. Your rights

Under the PDPA, you have the right to access personal data we hold about you, correct inaccurate personal data, and withdraw consent for data processing (subject to legal or contractual restrictions). To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@minloq.sg.

8. Cookies

We use only essential cookies necessary to operate the platform (session management and authentication). We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated to subscribers via email. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related queries or to exercise your rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at hello@minloq.sg.

Minloq provides AI-assisted extraction as a productivity tool. Users are solely responsible for verifying extracted data before approval. Minloq does not provide accounting, legal or tax advice.
